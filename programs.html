What we do

Four fronts, one mission

Environment is our root — and from it grows learning, safety and community care. Here's the work, in full.

🌿
01 / ENVIRONMENT

Greening Ahmedabad

Where DEV Foundation began — and never stopped. Trees planted on birthdays and every World Environment Day since 2011.

2011 → TODAY

53,000 trees planted & gifted

Around 53,000 trees put into the ground and into people's hands across the city — many gifted to citizens to plant and care for themselves. Our largest single drive came in the 2026 monsoon: over 3,000 saplings in one week.

🌳 ~53,000 trees📍 Ahmedabad🎁 Gifted to citizens

03 / CHILD SAFETY

Prepared, not scared

Giving children the knowledge to protect themselves — from fire emergencies to record-breaking summer heat.

RECENT · 6 MONTHS

Fire safety for 300 children

With a fire-safety expert, we trained 300 orphaned children in exactly what to do if danger strikes — run in three batches of 100 over six months, so every child got real attention.

🚒 300 children🗓 3 batches / 6 months👩‍🚒 Expert-led

2026 · SUMMER

Heat-stroke awareness seminar

During Ahmedabad's record-breaking summer, we taught children to recognise heat stroke and keep themselves cool and safe — practical safety for a warming world.
